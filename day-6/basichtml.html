Hawassa University

Hawassa University is one of the prominent medical universities in Ethiopia, offering programs in medicine, anesthesia, laboratory sciences, pharmacy, and nursing.

Fields available

  • Medicine
  • Anesthesia
  • Medical Laboratory
  • Pharmacy
  • Nursing & Veterinary (related programs)
